# K-Pop Age Master: Guess the Age

A fun and interactive web-based quiz game that challenges you to guess the age of your favorite K-Pop idols!

## Features

- **Dynamic Questions:** A wide range of K-Pop idols with their ages calculated as of 2026.
- **Interactive Gameplay:** Guess the age from a set of four options, with instant feedback on your choice.
- **Scoring System:** Keep track of your score as you progress through the quiz.
- **Seamless Replay:** The "Play Again" button lets you restart the quiz instantly without a full page reload.
- **Sleek and Modern UI:** A visually appealing interface with a dark theme and neon aesthetics.

## How to Play

1.  Open the `index.html` file in your web browser.
2.  The quiz will start automatically.
3.  For each question, you will see an Instagram post of a K-Pop idol.
4.  Choose the age you think is correct from the four options provided.
5.  See if you were right and move on to the next question!
6.  At the end of the quiz, you'll see your final score.

Have fun playing!
