About Us

Our Story

Welcome to K-Pop Idol Age Guessr! We are a passionate team of K-Pop fans who wanted to create a fun and engaging way for fellow fans to test their knowledge of idol ages.

Our journey began with a simple idea: create a game that is both challenging and enjoyable for K-Pop enthusiasts of all levels. We spend countless hours researching and updating our database to ensure the ages are accurate and reflect the current date.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a fun, interactive, and informative platform for the K-Pop community. We believe that games like ours can bring fans closer together and foster a deeper appreciation for the artists we admire.

Meet the Team

We are developers, designers, and content creators united by our love for K-Pop. We are dedicated to continuously improving the site and adding new features to enhance your experience.
